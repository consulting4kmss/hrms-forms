Drug and Alcohol Testing Information Request(Must Ask Question)-DOT Safety-Sensitive Position 49 CFR 40.25(j)

In accordance with 49 CFR Part 40.25(), Dominion Energy must ask all candidates seeking employment in a Safety- Sensitive Position as defined by Department of Transportation (DOT) rules if they have tested positive or refused to test on any pre-employment drug test to which the employee applied for, but did not obtain, that was covered under DOT drug testing rules. The period of the "must ask question is TWO (2) years. The must ask question applies to new hire candidates and to existing employee transfer candidates whose job duties after hire or transfer will involve DOT safety-sensitive work.

Please fill out information below and return with the other background forms for DOT covered positions.

If the answer to the "must ask" question is affirmative (have tested positive or refused to test), the new hire or transferring employee may not work in a Dominion Energy safety-sensitive position until and unless the new hire / transfer employee documents successful completion of the DOT return-to-duty process. Send DOT return-to -duty documentation to DominionEHS@dominionenergy.com.

For questions regarding an affirmative response, contact the Dominion Drug and Alcohol Hotline at 804-771-3020, Option #2.

"Have you tested positive, or refused to test, on any pre-employment drug or alcohol test administered by an employer to which the employee applied for, but did not obtain, safety-sensitive transportation work covered by DOT agency drug and alcohol testing rules during the past TWO years."
