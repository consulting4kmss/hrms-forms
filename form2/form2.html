Driver's Rights Concerning Investigation Information

You have the following rights regarding the investigative information that will be provided to Dominion Energy from your previous employers:

1. The right to review information provided by previous employers.

2. The right to have errors in the information corrected by the previous employer and for that previous employer to re-send the corrected information to the prospective employer.

3. The right to have a rebuttal statement attached to the alleged erroneous information, if the previous employer and the driver cannot agree on the accuracy of the information.

4. Drivers who have previous Department of Transportation regulated employment history in the preceding three years and wish to review previous employer provided investigative information must submit a written request to the prospective employer, which may be done at any time, including when applying, or as late as 30 days after being employed or being notified of denial of employment. The prospective employer must provide this information to the applicant within five (5) business days of receiving the written request. If the prospective employer has not yet received the requested information from the previous employer(s), then the five-business days deadline will begin when the prospective employer receives the requested safety performance history information. If the driver has not arranged to pick up or receive the requested records within thirty (30) days of the prospective employer making them available, the prospective motor carrier may consider the driver to have waived his/her request to review the records.

5. A CDL driver who believes a Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse record for their license is incorrect may petition the FMCSA to correct the error.

PRE-EMPLOYMENT/PRE-TRANSFER CONSENT FOR FEDERAL MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY ADMINISTRATION (FMCSA) DRUG & ALCOHOL CLEARINGHOUSE QUERY

FMCSR Section 382.701 requires that holders of a commercial driver's license (CDL) or commercial learner's permit (CLP) who are changing employers or who are transferring from a non-driving position into a position that requires operating a commercial motor vehicle give specific consent for a Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse query. This requires action on your part to register in the Clearinghouse and give electronic consent in the Clearinghouse for the pre-employment query when requested by Dominion Energy. Failure to immediately register your CDL or CLP in the Clearinghouse at www.clearinghouse.fmcas.dot.gov will delay your background investigation. If hired, you will be subject to an annual Clearinghouse query to verify that you remain qualified as a CDL-licensed driver.

As part of this application and safety performance background investigation, you must provide the following consent and acknowledgement

FMCSA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse Limited Query Consent [49 CFR 382.701] - This is my general consent for Dominion Energy or its authorized service agent to access and conduct a limited query in the FMCSA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse for any violations of drug and alcohol prohibitions in 49 CFR 382 Subpart B, including positive drug or alcohol test results, return to duty/follow-up test results, adulterations, and refusals. Additionally, I acknowledge that as part of my pre-employment or pre-transfer background investigation, I must register as a CDL or CLP driver in the Clearinghouse and give my specific electronic consent in the Clearinghouse when requested by Dominion Energy to obtain a full query into my Clearinghouse record. I understand that if I fail to give the above general and specific consent to Dominion Energy, I cannot operate any commercial motor vehicle for the company and may no longer meet the essential function requirements of my job. This general consent for DACH limited query is for my term of employment with as a commercial motor vehicle driver for Dominion Energy.

Authorization for Service Agent - As part of becoming a FMCSA-regulated driver for Dominion Energy, I must complete a driver background investigation including a release authorization for the company or company service agent to obtain consumer reports related to my qualifications as driver. This is my authorization for Dominion Energy or Dominion Energy authorized service agent to obtain my motor vehicle record and my FMSCA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse record, if applicable, for purpose of verifying driver qualification and managing my FMCSArequired driver qualification file. This authorization is for my term of employment as a commercial motor vehicle driver for the company.
